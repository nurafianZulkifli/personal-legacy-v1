School Experience

By: Nurafian Zulkifli    Date: August 31st, 2019

The new Polytechnic semester has started. While my body and self is slowly adapting to the fast-paced environment, I actually enjoyed my time here in Singapore Polytechnic. Not all things are great of course. The pacing of the school term is way too fast and I ended up not having the time to rest.

Modules like Mathematics and Programming are a pain, as I was not good at it in the first place. These resulted in my Semester 1's GPA being not as good as I have hoped for, but oh well. There's always time to buck up.
