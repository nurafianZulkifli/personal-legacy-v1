Done and Dusted

By: Nurafian Zulkifli    Date: February 19th, 2019

The 2 years in ITE is up. I barely had time to digest that fact as I really had fun. ITE has given me the chance to prove that I am actually capable of doing something well.

The skills obtained, such as being able to code a simple Android app from scratch, learning how to fix and troubleshoot a computer, as well as some programming fundamentals really helped me to be a much more useful person. With these skills at my fingertips, I hope that I can expand my skills even more, so that it can be turned into a profession? Who knows?
