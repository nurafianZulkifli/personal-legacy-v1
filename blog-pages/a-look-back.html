A look back at 2019

By: Nurafian Zulkifli    Date: December 31st, 2019

A lot has happened this year, be it positively or negatively. This year is probably the most significant as I've officially graduated from ITE in February 2019, and I then started my Polytechnic Years in April.

In addition, this year is also deemed to be my worst year as I've lost a family member which I was closed to. Despite that, I continued to persevere on, and continued the year as usual. In a nutshell, 2019 has been a year of awards which I've obtained during my time in ITE. Hard work pays off I guess?
