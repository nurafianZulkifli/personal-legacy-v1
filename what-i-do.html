What I Do

Things I do during my free time

  Being a Gadget Enthusiast

I'm always intrigued to try out different smartphones that comes out. I always believed in not sticking to one brand, as other phone brands may have something special too. That made me interested to change phones every few months (whenever I have the means to.)


  Selling my used smartphone

Once a new smartphone comes out, and I have enough money, I would then sell the phone I have used. Selling it is never easy as I have to do the necessary preparations like cleaning it, ensure that there's no damage and also to ensure that the phone's condition is pristine enough to be sold.
